Wednesday 8th March 1995

They can finally have some time to theselves to slow down and appreciate the person they fell in love with all those years ago. They can reminise on the wacky hijinks they got up to in their youths, and really rekindle the spark of their relationship that had been long since put out by the overwhelming pressures of their lives. Or they're just 2 birds sat next to eachother hahaha
