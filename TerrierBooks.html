The Cold Zone by Nicholas Prescott

Step into the exciting world of Beasts, Monsters, and Mythological Wonders in this Exciting disection of stories and legends about the Yeti. Is it just an old wives tale, or do we share this spinning planet we call Earth with fantasies never before discovered?

Tulip Crazy by Steven Queen

After moving to a new town, everything is looking up for the Smith family. Until one day they hear about the old tulip fields... Steven Queen's latest horror thriller follows the Smith Family as they uncover the secrets in this otherwise idyllic town.

The Journey Ahead by David Pezz

Based on a true story, The Journey Ahead by David Pezz follows one man as he navigates the merciless power of the ocean on his sailboat. Pushing through food shortages, perilous sea creature, and worst of all, his own mind...

Low Fidelity by Rick Carhorn

Waiter by day, Secret Spy by night. All Daisy Mariott can trust is that no matter who gets in the way, justice must be served hot. Join Daisy is this high stakes mission to topple the shadowy service industry using her wit, her pent up anger, and her black belt in judo.
